Origins

Anime started in Japan in the early 20th century, evolving from simple short films to a major entertainment industry by the 1960s. Osamu Tezuka, known as the "God of Manga," influenced early anime style.

Modern Anime

Today, anime includes TV shows, movies, and online streaming, gaining fans worldwide with genres ranging from action to romance.
